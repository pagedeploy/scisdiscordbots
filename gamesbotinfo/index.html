Information

This bot lets you play rock-paper-scissors, tic-tac-toe, bowling, flip a coin, guess a number, and use an 8 ball directly on Discord!

Note: Most of the games (see below) require you to vote on top.gg before using them.

Games

Type /games and choose from one of the following games to play.

Rock Paper Scissors

Battle the bot in an simple game of Rock Paper Scissors.

Tic Tac Toe

Challenge a friend to a friendly game of Tic Tac Toe.

Bowling

Aim your bowling ball by choose the angle and power to hit pins.

8 Ball

Ask the 8 Ball any yes/no question you want an answer for.

Coinflip

Test your luck with a coinflip. Will it be heads or tails?

Guess a Number

Try to guess the secret number within 3 tries.

Miscellaneous

Type /misc for all the other commands Games Bot has to offer, including the support server, bot credits, and server count.
